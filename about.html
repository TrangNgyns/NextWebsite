About us

Welcome to NEXT, the dynamic student-run agency born from Humber College’s esteemed Faculty of Media and Creative Arts. Formerly known as Building F, we’ve evolved to embody a fresh and innovative approach to bridging academic learning with real-world application.

Our Evolution

NEXT represents a new chapter in our journey. Since 2016, we’ve been dedicated to offering students real-world experience through meaningful projects with external organizations. Now, as a fully operational student-run agency, we’re pushing the boundaries of what’s possible, blending academic rigor with industry practice to deliver exceptional results.

Our Values

We are driven by our core values of Education, Creativity, Collaboration, Fun, and Freshness. These principles guide our approach to every project, ensuring that we not only meet but exceed expectations. At NEXT, we believe in the power of learning through doing, and we’re committed to fostering a collaborative environment that inspires both our students and our clients.
